About Coldwell Banker® Costa Rica

Our Brand

Coldwell Banker® Costa Rica features (9) franchise offices located in the most popular Costa Rica destinations.



Our People

Our brokers are highly trained experts in Costa Rica real estate transactions ensuring a safe and smooth purchase or sale. Make your real estate investment a memorable and safe experience working with one of our elite professionals.



Our Technology

ColdwellBankerCostaRica.com is the most comprehensive website for searching residential and commercial properties in Costa Rica. We use the most advanced real estate listing management software in the world giving our clients the most up-to-date selection of properties for sale and rent. The technology also allows us to promote our clients’ properties instantly across a global network of real estate portals. Our Commitment

Integrity and professionalism are essential for any real estate transaction. Costa Rica does not have many of the same legislative protections in place for real estate buyers and sellers making it crucial to work with a qualified and honest broker who will tell it like it is. We are committed to showing you the properties that meet your criteria and providing professional representation, customer service, and intangible resources that exceed your expectations.
